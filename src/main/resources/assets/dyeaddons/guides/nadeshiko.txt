Author: Anlg
How to obtain:
Nadeshiko dye has a chance to appear once as a pair in the experimentation table's superpairs experiment.
Nadeshiko dye has a base 1/25,000 chance to appear in a metaphysical experiment.
There is a rng meter for this dye to guarantee a dye after 2,500,000 progress. It is very unlikely to drop the dye before reaching 100% meter.

Method:
Do your experiments four times per day. Try to get 100k+ enchanting xp pairs to fill your meter faster.
