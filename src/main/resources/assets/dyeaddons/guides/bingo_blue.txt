How to obtain:
Bingo Blue dye can be purchased from the bingo shop for 500 bingo points and requires Bingo Rank III to purchase. The bingo shop is open 3 days before and after every bingo event. You can only obtain bingo points from participating in the bingo event.
During a normal bingo event, you can get 85 bingo points from completing goals and getting every line, and up to 75 bingo points by getting top 1% in every community goal.
It is recommended to get the max bingo rank before saving bingo points for the dye.
