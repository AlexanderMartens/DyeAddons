How to obtain:
Emerald dye rolls when breaking emerald ore or pure emerald on mining islands.
Emerald dye has a base 1/5,000,000 chance to drop.
Blocks broken from mining spread do roll for the dye. Mining fortune does not affect the chance of the dye.

Method:
The best place to mine emeralds is the veins on the right side of the great ice wall in the dwarven mines.

Gear:
- 4/4 Glossy Mineral
- Lustrous Reforge if Mining Fiesta

Hotm:
- Max Efficient Miner
- Sheer Force
