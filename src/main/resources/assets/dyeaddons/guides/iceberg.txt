How to Obtain:
Iceberg dye rolls when you kill any winter sea creature. Only the killer of the sea creature rolls for the dye. If you fish a sea creature and someone else kills it, you will not roll for the dye; only the person that killed the sea creatures can drop the dye.
Iceberg dye has a base of 1/2,500,000 chance from common and uncommon mobs, 1/500,000 chance from rare and epic mobs, and 1/50,000 chance from legendary and mythic mobs.
Like most dyes dropped from mobs, iceberg dye is affected by magic find and looting. Shurikens, kill combo, and wolf fur mixin do not increase magic find on this dye.
Note: Reindrakes cannot drop iceberg dye.

Method:
Try to find a lobby with a lot of people fishing to increase bobbin' time buff. Some people barn fish in the hot springs, so you can fish there and also kill their sea creatures.
You are most likely to drop the dye from a grinch than a yeti, so it is important to kill with high magic find.

Gear:
- 4/4 Magma Lord Armor with bobbin' time 5
- Rift Necklace, David's Cloak, Bloodshot Gillsplash Belt, Gillsplash Gloves
- Clover Helmet + 3/4 Sorrow with renowned and bobbin' time 5
- Giant Rod or Rod of the Sea
- Baby Yeti pet with Burnt Texts
- Golden Dragon pet with Hephaestus Relic

Pet Rules:
- On throw fishing hook -> Baby Yeti
- On kill sea creature -> Golden Dragon
