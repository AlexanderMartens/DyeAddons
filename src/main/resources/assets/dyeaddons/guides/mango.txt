How to obtain:
Mango dye rolls for every log you break.
Mango dye has a base 1/10,000,000 chance to drop per log.
Logs broken from sweep can drop the dye. Foraging fortune does not increase the chance of dropping the dye. It is unknown if additional logs broken from the tree breaking can drop the dye.

Method:
The fastest way to get the dye is breaking fig trees in galatea. The best way to cut fig trees is to only cut the large fig trees. Jump and break logs at the base of the tree, then etherwarp up to the branches and break each branch. If you are fast enough, the next tree should regenerate shortly before you arrive, so there is little chance of being griefed by other players.
