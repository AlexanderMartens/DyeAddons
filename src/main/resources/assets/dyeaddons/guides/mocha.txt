Author: Anlg
How to obtain:
Mocha dye rolls from brewing potions.
Mocha dye has a base chance of 1/100,000,000 to 100,000 depending on the level of the potion.

Method:
The best potions to make for this dye is level 7-8 regeneration or instant health potions. Each batch of 3 potions needs one nether wart, one enchanted ghast tear or enchanted glistering melon, and one enchanted glowstone (block or dust). Use a witch pet to reduce how long it takes potions to brew by 50%.
Enchanted ghast tears can be obtained from either bazaar, ghast minions, or buy ghast tears from the alchemist, An from the barbarian faction, and Ulyn from the mage faction.
Enchanted glowstone can be obtained from the crimson isle. The best spot to mine is the vein near the hellwisps.
The autobrewer design can be found on my island (Anlg - Zucchini). There are also signs on how to operate the brewer.

Brewing potions consists of 4 steps:
- Buy empty water bottles from the alchemist
- Fill empty water bottles with a water source. Bind your use item keybind to a keyboard key and hold it down to fill bottles faster.
- Put water bottles into the top storage. Bind /pickupstash to a key and hold down q to drop bottles into hoppers above chests.
- Claim potions from the bottom brewing stand. Shift click the potions from the first slot in the brewing stand and hold q in the trades menu to sell quickly. Also bind /trades to a key.
