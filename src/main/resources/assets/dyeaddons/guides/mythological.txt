Author: MorFFFFF and Anlg
How to obtain:
Mythological dye rolls when killing mythological creatures. Only the killer of the mythological creature rolls for the dye. If you spawn a mythological creature and someone else kills it, you will not roll for the dye; only the person that killed the mythological creature can drop the dye.
Mythological dye has a base chance from 1/1,000,000 to 1/10,000 depending on the rarity of the mob.
Like most dyes dropped from mobs, mythological dye is affected by magic find and looting. Shurikens, kill combo, and wolf fur mixin do not increase magic find on this dye.

Method:
You need a party of 6 people where you will be killing all legendary and mythic mobs. The easiest way is to create a party in sbo mod /sbopf and indicate that you are the killer, and kick anyone who disagrees. Make sure everyone in the party does not have fire aspect and venomous on their weapon as it can steal the kill.

- On kill mythological mob -> Golden Dragon
