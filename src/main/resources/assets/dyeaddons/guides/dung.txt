Author: Anlg
How to obtain:
Dung dye rolls from killing pests on the garden.
Dung dye has a base 1/250,000 chance from kill pests, and a 1/50,000 chance from killing field mice and lunar moths.
Dung dye is affected by overbloom.

Method:
It is best to use moondew as bait in traps because they have a 100% chance of capturing a lunar moth. You an also open traps with a mosquito pet to reduce the cooldown of the trap on the next pest by 20%.

Gear:
- 4/4 Helianthus Armor with sunset 5 and pesterminator 6
- Rose Dragon pet with Poignant Lucky Clover
- Rarefinder chip level 20
- Crop Bug 10
- Pest Luck 10
- Freshly baked heirloom
- Finn's Focaccia (during grand feast)
