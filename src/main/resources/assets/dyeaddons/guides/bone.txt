Author: Anlg
How to obtain:
Bone dye rolls every time you kill a skeletal mob. This includes all mobs with the skeletal mob type, as well as skeleton spawned from minions.
Bone dye has a base 1/3,000,000 chance to drop from skeletal mobs.
Like most dyes dropped from mobs, bone dye is affected by magic find and looting. Shurikens, kill combo, and wolf fur mixin do not increase magic find on this dye.

Method:
The best way to get this dye is to kill skeletons spawned from minions on your private island. You can funnel mobs spawned from minions with water into a small chamber to kill. You can use a weapon with aoe ability to kill all the skeletons at once. Make sure you are holding a weapon with high magic find and looting when the skeletons die.
Note: Afking with toggle break/use item is against hypixel rules.

Gear:
- Clover Helmet + 3/4 Sorrow with renowned reforge
- Golden Dragon pet with Hephaestus Relic
- Any weapon with Chimera 5, Looting 5, and Divine Gift 3
