How to obtain:
Pelt dye rolls from killing trapper animals in the mushroom desert.
Pelt dye has a base chance from 1/250,000 to 1/10,000 depending on the rarity of the animal.
Unlike most dyes dropped from mobs. Pelt dye is not affected by magic find or looting.

Method:
Try to kill your trapper animal before Trevor's cooldown expires. Also bind /call trevor to a key. Make sure you don't get too close to elusive animals because they will despawn.
Don't wear clover helmet because that will reduce your tracking.
