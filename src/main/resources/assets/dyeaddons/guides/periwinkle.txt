Author: Extrembuilders and telyv
How to obtain:
Periwinkle dye rolls from killing runic mobs.
Periwinkle dye has a base chance of 1/500,000 to 1/100,000 depending on the level of the mob.
Like most dyes dropped from mobs, periwinkle dye is affected by magic find and looting. Shurikens, kill combo, and wolf fur mixin do not increase magic find on this dye.

Methods:
- Grind Ghosts since you can kill them with a very High Magic find setup
- Kill Spiders in the Spiders since there are a ton of mobs (silverfishes)
- Cycle lobbies through dragons nest, hub crypt and hub castle as these also have a lot of mobs to potentially be runic, alongside that if you are doing secret runs for secret dye you could progress past maxor in the floor 7 boss fight and kill all of the skeletons at the storm phase, there is usually a few runics aswell as boosting your chance for bone dye
