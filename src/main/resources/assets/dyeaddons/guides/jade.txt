Author: Anlg
How to obtain:
Jade dye rolls from crystal nucleus bundles.
Jade dye has a base 1/500,000 chance to replace each item roll of a crystal nucleus bundle.
You can increase how many item rolls in your crystal nucleus bundle with mole pet, high roller perk, and bigger box attribute

Method:
Find a lobby that has a low day counter with most crystals close to the nucleus and ideally King Yolkar on the way to the amber crystal.
Jade Crystal - Get tools before or during runs. Use mole pet to increase chances of tools and a mod for treasure waypoints.
Sapphire Crystal - The best way to grind precursor apparatus is from automatons in the lost precursor city. Use mole pet and any looting 4 weapon. Place a waypoint at every automaton location you find because they will always spawn in the same spot in a lobby.
Amethyst Crystal - There isn't a known way to skip the jungle temple. The best way to grind jungle keys is to mine hardstone with a jungle pickaxe and buy jungle keys from Odawa.
Amber Crystal - Either kill goblins in the dwarven mines or mine for treasure chests in the goblin holdout
Topaz Crystal - Kill bal with a terminator, terror armor, and using the wither cloak ability. You can speed up the spawning animation by entering and leaving the Khazad-dûm 3-4 times. Do this by digging a 6 block hole under bal and jumping.
