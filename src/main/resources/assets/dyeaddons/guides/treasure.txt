How to obtain:
Treasure dye rolls when fishing treasure.
Treasure dye has a base 1/1,000,000 chance from good catches, 1/100,000 chance from great catches, and 1/10,000 chance from outstanding catches.

Method:
The best place to fish is the backwater bayou. Stand inside a hotspot for tiki mask buff and treasure chance hotspot if available.

- Lvl 100 Hermit Crab w/ 5 nearby people using same pet
